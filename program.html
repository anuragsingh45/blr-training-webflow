12-Day Onboarding Program

Comprehensive training to transform you into a successful Relationship Manager

Training Journey

Day-by-Day Breakdown

Structured learning path from induction to independent meetings

01

Company Induction & Foundation

September 1, 2025

Understand DigitalBuzz's vision, your role within the company structure, and the basic rules of engagement.

Morning Session (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM)

  • Welcome by HR Manager Ankita and Senior HR Ragini
  • Company policies walkthrough
  • Office tour and familiarization
  • IT orientation for email, CRM, and internal software

Afternoon Session (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

  • Introduction to DigitalBuzz and our core business
  • Meet the departments and key personnel
  • Company process and workflow overview
  • Rules, regulations, and career path discussion
02

Mastering the Bangalore Market

September 2, 2025

Develop a deep understanding of the Bangalore real estate landscape, including key locations, major developers, and client profiles.

Key Learning Areas

  • Bangalore Market & Connectivity Map (North, East, South, West)
  • A-Grade Developers & Their Projects (Prestige, Sobha, Brigade, Godrej)
  • The Channel Partner (CP) Profile and how to approach them
03

Sales Pitch & Calling

September 3, 2025

Learn the DigitalBuzz cold calling pitch and scripts. Focus on the "Straight Line Method" to establish authority in the first 30 seconds.

Training Components

  • Cold calling pitch structure and key talking points
  • Straight Line Method for establishing authority quickly
  • Supervised live calls to potential clients
  • Practice sessions with feedback from trainers
04-05

F2F Meeting Module Training

September 4-5, 2025

Detailed walkthrough of the face-to-face (F2F) meeting process including pre-meeting research, conversation structure, and strategy presentation.

Key Training Areas

  • Pre-meeting research and preparation
  • Structuring the conversation from greeting to close
  • Effective strategy presentation techniques
  • Company policies discussion framework
06

Mock Sessions

September 6, 2025

Conduct mock F2F meetings with trainers and senior RMs playing the role of the client. Receive constructive feedback on your presentation, communication, and objection handling skills.

Session Details

  • Multiple mock meeting scenarios with different client types
  • Immediate feedback on performance
  • Opportunity to refine techniques before live meetings
07-09

On-Field Meetings & Objection Handling

September 7-9, 2025

Accompany senior RMs on actual client meetings. Observe how they build rapport, present solutions, and handle real-world objections. Participate in post-meeting debriefs.

Learning Objectives

  • Observe experienced RMs in live client interactions
  • Learn effective objection handling techniques
  • Participate in post-meeting debriefs and analysis
10

Your First Meeting (Shadowed)

September 10, 2025

You will lead your own client meeting from start to finish. A senior RM or Team Lead will accompany you to observe and provide support if needed.

Key Aspects

  • Take the lead in all aspects of the client meeting
  • Senior RM observes and takes notes for feedback
  • Support available if needed, but minimal intervention
11

Independent Meetings

September 11, 2025

You will conduct your own client meetings independently. The focus is on applying all the training from the previous days in a live environment.

Expectations

  • Complete independence in conducting meetings
  • Apply all learned techniques and strategies
  • Self-evaluation after each meeting
12

Review Meeting

September 12, 2025

A comprehensive review of your performance with your Team Lead and the Sales Head. Discuss feedback, review understanding, and set performance goals.

Agenda

  • Discuss feedback from mock calls and on-field meetings
  • Review your understanding of products and processes
  • Address any remaining questions or challenges
  • Set clear performance goals for your first 30 days
