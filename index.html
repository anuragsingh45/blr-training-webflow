Key Regions

Bangalore's Prime Real Estate Areas

Strategic locations for real estate development and investment

North Bangalore

Hebbal, Yelahanka, Thanisandra, Devanahalli

Overview: Known for its proximity to the Kempegowda International Airport and major tech parks like Manyata.

Connectivity: Bellary Road (NH 44), Outer Ring Road (ORR), and the upcoming Metro Blue Line (Airport Line).

Key Drivers: Aerospace Park, Devanahalli Business Park, and KIADB Hardware Tech Park make it a hub for commercial and residential growth.

East Bangalore

Whitefield, Sarjapur Road, Marathahalli, Budigere Cross

Overview: The quintessential IT corridor of Bangalore, home to numerous tech parks and multinational companies.

Connectivity: Outer Ring Road (ORR), Whitefield Main Road, Sarjapur Road, and the operational Purple Metro Line.

Key Drivers: ITPL, EPIP Zone, and other tech parks drive housing demand. Excellent social infrastructure with malls, schools, and hospitals.

South Bangalore

Kanakapura Road, Bannerghatta Road, Electronic City, Begur

Overview: A well-established residential area combined with the major electronics industrial park, Electronic City.

Connectivity: Hosur Road, NICE Ring Road, Green Metro Line (Kanakapura Road), and the new Yellow Metro Line (Electronic City).

Key Drivers: Established residential appeal, major IT/ITES companies in Electronic City, and excellent connectivity to other parts of the city.

West Bangalore

Rajajinagar, Yeshwanthpur, Tumkur Road

Overview: A traditional part of Bangalore mixed with modern industrial and commercial development.

Connectivity: Well-connected by both the Green and Purple Metro Lines, Tumkur Road (NH 48), and proximity to Yeshwanthpur Railway Station.

Key Drivers: Home to the World Trade Center, Orion Mall, and major industrial areas, making it a balanced commercial and residential hub.
