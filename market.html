North Bangalore

Hebbal, Yelahanka, Devanahalli

Connectivity: Bellary Road (NH 44), upcoming Metro Blue Line.

New Launch (2025): Prestige Somerville (near Old Airport Road).

East Bangalore

Whitefield, Sarjapur Road, Marathahalli

Connectivity: Outer Ring Road (ORR), Purple Metro Line.

New Launch (2025): Godrej Woodscapes (Budigere Cross).

South Bangalore

Kanakapura Road, Bannerghatta Road

Connectivity: Green Metro Line, NICE Ring Road.

New Launch (2025): Brigade Sanctuary (off Sarjapur Road).

West Bangalore

Rajajinagar, Yeshwanthpur

Connectivity: Green and Purple Metro Lines.

New Launch (2025): Sobha Neopolis (near Panathur, Varthur).
